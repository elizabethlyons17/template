The purpose of this video is for the grandson of an Evangelist Oral Roberts to share a letter he wrote to his dead Uncle Ronnie. Ronnie was gay just like him and after he divorced his wife and was afraid of losing his kids he became depressed and wrote to his Uncle Ronnie who commited suicide about how it gets better. The video is of him reading part of the letter encouraging positivy and saying everyone is beautiful and deserves happiness. The video attempts to do this by making the message appear in a very powerful and passionate way. Before and after the reading there is writings giving details of the story. By doing this it makes the message feel more powerful. The structure of the message was readings before the oral story and then after he was done reading it concluded with more reading promoting the message that it gets better. I haven't finished the Lived Expierence project but the video helped me with how I will think about it and organize it. He drew from past expierence to create a narrative that flowed well and was organized cronlogically so it was easy to follow and interesting.
